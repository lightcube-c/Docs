What is LightCube?


We are made up of five students living in Chiba Prefecture who are working on the development of programming learning software for educational institutions to support programming education. LightCube was given a particularly meaningless name by one of the developers participating in the project.

{{% /blocks/lead %}} {{% blocks/lead %}}

Please cooperate!


LightCube welcomes those who cooperate in development, those who can help with translation work even if they can not program, those who donate!
